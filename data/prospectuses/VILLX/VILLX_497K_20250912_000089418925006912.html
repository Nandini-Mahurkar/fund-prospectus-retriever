Effective at the close of business on September 12, 2025 (the “Effective Date”), Anthony DeMeo will no longer serve as a portfolio manager of the Muzinich Dynamic Income Fund (the “Dynamic Income Fund” or the “Fund”). Accordingly, all references to Mr. DeMeo are removed. Additionally, as of the Effective Date, Eric Schure, CFA, serves as a portfolio manager of the Fund. Michael L. McEachern, Stuart Fuller, Joseph Galzerano, Warren Hyland, Thomas Samson and Corentin Tarlier will continue to serve as portfolio managers of the Fund.
